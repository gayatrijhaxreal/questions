# What is Quiz?

The TradeVed Quiz is a structured daily market intelligence system designed to:

- Build awareness

- Reinforce concepts

- Develop disciplined thinking

- Improve decision quality

It is not just a “quiz feature.”
It is a daily training mechanism for traders.
Instead of consuming random information, Quiz helps you:
Think like a trader — consistently.

# Why Daily Quizzes Matter in Trading

Trading is a decision-based activity.
Better decisions require:

- Updated market awareness
- Concept clarity
- Risk understanding
- Scenario thinking

Most traders learn passively.
Quiz forces active engagement.
When you answer a question:

- You test your Understanding
- You evaluate risk logic
- You reinforce knowledge
- You identify gaps immediately

This reduces:

- Blind confidence
- Superficial understanding
- Passive content consumption

Quiz turns learning into participation

# How the Quiz System Works

The Quiz engine operates in a structured format.

### 1️⃣ Daily Questions

Each day, users receive:

- Market-relevent questions
- Concept-based questions
- Scenario-based logic questions

Questions may be linked to:

- Current market events
- Risk management principles
- Strategy behavior
- Options payoff logic
- Market structure fundamentals

### 2️⃣ Immediate Feedback

After answering:

- Correct answer is explained
- Concept clarity is provided
- Risk perspective is highlighted

The goal is not just to score.
The goal is to understand.

### 3️⃣ XP & Streak System

Quiz includes:

- Experience Points (XP)
- Daily streak tracking
- Consistency rewards

Why this matters:
Trading discipline is built through repetition.
Consistency builds:

- Awareness
- Confidence
- Structured thinking

Missing days reduces streak — reinforcing habit accountability.
